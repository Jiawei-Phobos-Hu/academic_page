About

I am a Postdoc at Northwestern University Institute on Complex Systems (NICO). I work with Brian Uzzi at the Kellogg School of Management. I am interested in computational social science and data science. My research focuses on innovation, leadership, communication, social identity, and the intersections of these topics. My current research studies the mechanisms involved in the production and dissemination of novel ideas in science. I earned my PhD from University of Michigan School of Information (UMSI), where I was advised by Daniel M. Romero.
